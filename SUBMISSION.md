# SUBMISSION

## Project name
SyncBridge

## One-line description
An AI coordination workspace that turns cross-team communication into aligned execution.

## Short description
SyncBridge helps teams convert meetings, updates, documents, and stakeholder requests into structured execution plans, summaries, blockers, and launch coordination views.

## Problem
Large projects often involve product, engineering, design, operations, and stakeholders working across multiple channels. Important context gets fragmented across meetings, chats, docs, and status updates. This slows decisions, creates alignment gaps, and increases launch risk.

## Solution
SyncBridge is an AI coordination workspace for multi-team projects. It consolidates scattered project communication and transforms it into clear operational outputs such as stakeholder summaries, action items with owners, blocker tracking, launch readiness status, and roadmap coordination views.

## Why it stands out
Instead of being a generic AI chat wrapper, SyncBridge is framed as a project coordination layer for teams and multi-stakeholder work. The core value is operational clarity across many people, not just idea generation for one user.

## Why MiMo fits
MiMo is a strong fit because the product depends on summarization, action extraction, planning support, roadmap drafting, stakeholder communication, and cross-functional coordination outputs. These are all natural applications for a capable reasoning and planning model.
